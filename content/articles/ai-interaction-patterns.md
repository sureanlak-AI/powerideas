## PHASE 1: THE SHIFT

### 1. The Old Way vs The New Way

**Old way**: Forms, menus, buttons. The interface tells you what's possible. You fill in blanks.

**New way**: You describe intent, the AI interprets. That's harder, not easier.

> "Ya no se trata de hacer clic hasta llegar a una pantalla. Se trata de describir una intención y saber iterar sobre el resultado."

## PHASE 2: THE 6 PATTERNS

### 2. Wayfinders

Solves the blank page syndrome. The best AI apps don't start with an empty input. They give you a starting point, a suggestion, a "what about this?"

**Example**: Notion AI doesn't wait for your prompt. It suggests "Summarize this page" before you ask.

### 3. Prompt Actions & Tuners

**Prompt Actions** — The first result is a draft, not the final answer. Edit, don't accept.

**Tuners** — You don't configure font size anymore. You configure tone, style, depth. "Make it more casual" vs "Change typography to 14px".

The interface shifted from **what you see** to **what you mean**.

### 4. Variations, Trust & Governors

**Variations** — AI gives you a range, not THE answer. 3 options. Pick, combine, iterate.

**Trust Builders** — Citations, sources, confidence levels as UX features. "Here's where I got this."

**Governors** — You decide how much control to delegate. Auto-reply? Manual review? The slider is yours.
